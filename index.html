About us

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to leveraging the transformative potential of sports to make a positive impact on the lives of people. We passionately believe that the power of sports transcends the boundaries of the playing field, affecting change, fostering well-being, and promoting gender equality.

Welcome to Padimi Sports Foundation

Why Padimi Sports Foundation?

Padimi Sports Foundation stands out because of its unwavering commitment to creating a positive and lasting impact through sports. We are driven by the principles of equality, inclusion, and sustainability, and our programs are designed to reflect these values.

What we do

At Padimi Sports Foundation, we have undertaken a multifaceted approach to achieve our mission,
with a particular focus on contributing to the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) 3, 4, and 5.

Get Involved

We invite you to join us in our mission to make a difference through sports. Whether you're an athlete looking to inspire, a volunteer eager to make an impact, a donor with a vision, or a potential partner seeking to collaborate for change,
together we can create a world where sports become a driving force for personal growth, community development, and a brighter, more equitable future.
